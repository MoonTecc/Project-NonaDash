About NonaDash

Project NonaDash is a fast and efficient hand-tracking engine powered by YOLO. Control your cursor using simple hand gestures, with full support for gestures like fist. Designed for Windows, it brings intuitive motion control to your desktop experience.The latest Results can be downloaded below,

Features

Real-Time Hand Tracking

Detect your hand and gestures instantly with fast YOLO models.

Gesture Control

Use gestures like Fist to interact with your PC.

Fast Cursor Engine

Win32API-based cursor movement for smooth, less-lag control.

Flexable

Easily use-able on most of the OS!. Including, Linux and MacOS
